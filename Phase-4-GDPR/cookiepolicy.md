# Cookie Policy – Web-based Booking System

## Introduction
This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies in our system.

## What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small files stored on your device to enhance the user experience, store preferences, and track session information.

## Cookies We Use
- **Session Cookies:** Used to maintain login sessions and CSRF protection.
- **Functional Cookies:** Ensure proper functioning of booking forms.

## Managing Cookies
You can choose to disable cookies in your browser. Note that some features may not function properly without cookies.

## GDPR Compliance
- Cookies do not store sensitive personal data like passwords or birthdates.
- Users’ consent is obtained implicitly by using the system.
- Users may withdraw consent by clearing cookies or disabling them in the browser.
